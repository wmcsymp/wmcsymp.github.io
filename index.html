About the Symposium

The Computer Science Department at William & Mary (W&M) is hosting its second Graduate Studies Symposium! The symposium will consist of faculty and graduate student panels, research talks, poster session, social events, and more!

Learn everything you wanted to know about getting a CS PhD or MS degree at W&M:

  • Funded PhD and MS/PhD degrees
  • Cutting edge CS Research
  • Job Opportunities after an MS or PhD in CS
  • Life as a Graduate student at W&M
  • MS pathways for non-CS Majors

Venue and Parking

The W&M CS Symposium will take place at the College of William & Mary, in the Integrated Sciences Center (ISC) - (Auditorium ISC 1221)

Address: 540 Landrum Dr, Williamsburg, VA 23185

Here is a Google Maps link to the event site. https://maps.app.goo.gl/6aLTjjtSbKUiPGGe9 All the parking spots are along the Landrum Dr. and Ukrop Way. So, it is recommended to first navigate to the event site and find the next possible parking spot.

Due to a football game to happen on the same day of the symposium, participants can only park on the spots mapped below. Participants can park on faculty and staff spots, but NOT on "reserved" spots.

In case no parking spot is available, participants can also use off-campus commercial parking on their own cost (the cost is very low). Here is one of the closest parking garage: 230 N Henry St, Williamsburg, VA 23185
