W&M Computer Science Symposium for Graduate Studies

November 4, 2023 -- Williamsburg, Virginia

The symposium will be livestreamed on YouTube starting at 9:15 AM EDT: https://tinyurl.com/wmcsymp23

About the Symposium

The Computer Science Department at William & Mary (W&M) is hosting its second Graduate Studies Symposium! The symposium will consist of faculty and graduate student panels, research talks, poster session, social events, and more!

Learn everything you wanted to know about getting a CS PhD or MS degree at W&M:

  • Funded PhD and MS/PhD degrees
  • Cutting edge CS Research
  • Job Opportunities after an MS or PhD in CS
  • Life as a Graduate student at W&M
  • MS pathways for non-CS Majors

Travel Grants

We are offering partial travel grants to help students attend the symposium. If you would like to apply for a travel grant to attend, please register for the symposium, and indicate (on the registration form) your intent to apply for the travel grant, by the deadline below.

Important Dates

  • 5th October 2023 November 3rd, 5PM EST: Deadline to request a student travel grant
